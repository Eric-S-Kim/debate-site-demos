03The Protocol

A seven-move week,
gated by what ships.

Gold gears are judgment; teal moves carry the breadth; the red node is the gate that closes the week.

Day 02–3 hrs max

Pick one most-avoided, consequential deliverable (e.g. the Add Health R&R). Define “shipped” as a concrete, external output by Friday 5 pm. Freeze your tech stack — no new tools or refactors. Create a Week Log: anchor progress, daily briefs, keep / adapt / discard.

ConfigureDevices

Uninstall or hide competing apps, set GStack as defaults on phone and computer, and clarify your account strategy — personal vs. work.

Daily BriefAM · 20 min

Frame today’s anchor move, identify email and calendar risks, note life constraints, define one daily output, and protect your energy.

Deep WorkDaily · 90–150 min

Use GStack’s judgment gears — /office-hours, /plan-ceo-review, /codex, /investigate — exclusively on the anchor deliverable: frame, produce, adversarial review, cut, commit.

Thin LayerThroughout

All other emails, tasks, life admin and mobile capture enter GStack only via lightweight capture → triage → prepare → close loops. No deep redesign.

Failure DrillMid-week

Deliberately test GStack on messy cases. Classify the cause of each failure — retrieval, reasoning, prompt, or context — and define a fix or a kill.

Ship & DecideFriday · 5 pm

Submit or send the anchor deliverable. Write a one-page decision brief: 3–6 GStack patterns to keep, ideas to adapt, what to discard, and new rules & boundaries.

04Guardrails

Hold these the
whole week.

01

Define “shipped” concretely for your anchor (e.g. “sent to coauthors”) — not just “progress made.”

02

Prioritize GStack as a judgment system — reframing, adversarial review — over a deep operations system this week.

03

Don’t let GStack increase screen time, reduce movement, or worsen sleep. It should make you less fragmented.

04

Clarify privacy & account boundaries: what data (academic, health) can freely enter, what must be redacted, or stays local.

05

This is primarily an extraction test — what to port — not a full migration test.

06

Utility lives mainly in the 4 judgment-shaped gears (/office-hours, /plan-ceo-review, /codex, /investigate) — not the code-specific ones.

05Stress Test
What would prove this wrong
If your manuscript submission count actually rose in the six months after building your existing stack, the goal-displacement worry is falsified — making the output-gate potentially unnecessary overhead.
