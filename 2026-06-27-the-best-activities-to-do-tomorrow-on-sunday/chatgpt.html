Action plan

Build a calm Monday before it arrives.

The winning plan protects the day from both extremes: no frantic errand marathon, no screen-soaked drift.

Morning Ritual

Wake naturally, move your body (30-60 mins), then spend 30-45 mins planning top 3 week priorities and dumping mental clutter.

Practical Prep

Dedicate 60-90 mins to 1-2 high-impact, low-effort weekly tasks, such as laundry, light meal prep, or tidying main spaces.

Genuine Recharge

Block out 2-3 hours for a truly enjoyable, screen-free, non-work activity, such as a hobby, nature, reading, or meaningful social connection.

Early Wind-Down

Disconnect from screens 2 hours before bed, briefly preview Monday, and prioritize sufficient sleep.

Caveats: what to avoid

  • Avoid over-scheduling, treating Sunday like another workday, or succumbing to passive digital distractions that drain rather than recharge.
  • Do not leave all high-impact weekly preparation until Monday morning.
