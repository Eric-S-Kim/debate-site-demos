Honest caveats

Expect a different feel. Any truly mineral sunscreen will behave differently than your previous hybrid EltaMD.

Greenwashing. Not all "natural" or "organic-leaning" sunscreens are equal — verify active filters and certifications (EWG, Ecocert).

Tinted vs. untinted. Some highly-rated mineral formulas add a "universal" tint to reduce white cast — verify if you truly want untinted.

Botanical sensitizers. Many "natural" sunscreens use botanical oils (avocado, raspberry seed) that can irritate sensitive skin.

Reapplication is key. SPF requires generous application and reapplication every 2 hours — or after sweating or swimming.
