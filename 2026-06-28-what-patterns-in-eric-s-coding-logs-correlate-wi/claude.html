WHY IT HOLDS

Three pathways the advisors converged on.

R/01

Late Coding Disrupts Recovery

Late-night coding directly impairs sleep timing, duration, and consistency, and elevates next-day resting heart rate — all key physiological inputs to WHOOP Age. (Claude's emphasis.)

R/02

Movement Drives WHOOP Age

Cardiovascular fitness — RHR, VO₂ max, steps, strain — is the primary direct driver. Coding's best role is to enable, not displace consistent daily activity. (ChatGPT's point, later adopted by Claude.)

R/03

System-Building Reduces Chaos

Durable infrastructure work lowers future cognitive load and urgent late-night tasks — protecting early shutdowns and steady movement. (ChatGPT's updated view, refined by Gemini.)

THE PROTOCOL

A five-step plan to test the box.

01

Validate & Export WHOOP Data

Confirm WHOOP Age ownership; export daily WHOOP Age, Pace of Aging, RHR, HRV, sleep metrics, strain, steps, VO₂ max, and off-wrist flags.

02

Build Daily Coding Dataset

Extract daily last-active timestamp, min active after 21:30/22:00, total active time, longest session, and system-build vs. bug-fix tags.

03

Analyze Daily Mediators

Model coding features (esp. last-active & minutes after 9:30 PM) against next-day sleep consistency, duration, RHR, steps/strain. Include alcohol/illness as covariates.

04

Run a 21-Day Experiment

Enforce a strict no commits after 21:30 rule plus a non-negotiable daily movement block for three weeks.

05

Monitor & Decide

Track RHR, sleep consistency, sleep debt, steps, and Pace of Aging daily. Keep the rule if clear gains appear without output quality collapsing.

RISKS & FALSIFICATION

What could break
this read.

C1WHOOP Age data is sparse (n ≈ 4) for direct statistical analysis — focus on daily mediators for robust conclusions.
C2Confounders — alcohol, illness, stress, non-coding screen time — can skew apparent coding effects.
C3WHOOP algorithm recalibration or new-user settling can create non-behavioral "drops" in Healthspan metrics.
C4Reverse causation: feeling physiologically good might enable more system-building, not the other way around.
✕What would prove this wrong

Verified daily data showing late-night coding consistently improves WHOOP Age or Pace of Aging — even after controlling for sleep, RHR, movement, and all other external factors — would fundamentally challenge the primacy of timing.
